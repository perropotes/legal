# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-03

This privacy policy describes how the App handles information.

## 1. Data Collected
The App does not collect, store, or share personal data from any third party. The App operates exclusively with the personal accounts and credentials of its owner, Luis Miguel Gil.

## 2. Credentials and Tokens
API tokens and credentials required to publish to third-party platforms (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok) are stored locally on the owner's machine in environment files that are never transmitted, shared, or backed up to any external service.

## 3. Third-Party Platforms
When the App publishes content to a third-party platform, that platform's own privacy policy applies to the published content. The App does not transmit any data to any party other than the platform receiving the publish request.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking
The App does not use cookies, analytics, or any form of user tracking.

## 5. Changes
This policy may be updated at any time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
